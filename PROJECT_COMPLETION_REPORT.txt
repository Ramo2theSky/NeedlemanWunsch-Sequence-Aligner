   Expected: Same identity %, lower score (higher penalty effect)
   Result: More conservative alignment selection
   Use: When distinguishing subtle differences is critical

   Expected: Same identity %, lower score (lower rewards/penalties)
   Result: More permissive alignment selection
   Use: When prioritizing alignment coverage over stringency

   Expected: Same identity %, potentially higher score
   Result: More gaps allowed in alignment
   Use: Rare cases where indels should not be penalized

most DNA/RNA sequence alignment tasks.
